Hello, I"m Peter.

Who?

My name is Peter McCrae-Hokenson and I am a software developer living in Minneapolis, MN and working all over. As an art major out in the world, I’m compelled by:

  • Code that is as beautiful as it is air-tight
  • Websites that are both visually engaging and rich in content
  • Collaborating with developers who are excited about taking risks and having fun with the design process

a bit more...

Throughout my undergraduate wanderings I oscillated between three fields: Economics, Computer Science, and Studio Art. While my academic focal point was a moving target, my motivations never wavered. I was (and still am) searching for the right vehicle to create experiences that connect people with the opportunities and resources they seek.

During my senior year, I studied in Egypt, Tanzania, India, China, and Argentina. The impact these experiences had on me could not be overstated. After graduating, I was selected as one of three Fifth Year Emerging Artists for my work as a photographer and filmmaker. What came next was a year of enthusiastic art-making sponsored by St. Olaf College. Here is some of my work!

Now that I turn my gaze away from academia, I am excited about the intersection of art and technology. Anywhere where these two fields cross paths energizes me. If you are on the lookout for a developer who is equal parts meticulous and creative, I’d love to chat. You can find me at any of the links below.
